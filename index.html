Hi, I'm Roya!

I'm a freelance artist, Illustration major, and Animation minor at Otis College of Art and Design.

I was formerly studying animation at Diablo Valley College for two years until transferring to Otis. I like to challenge myself and take on new styles that I'm not particularly familiar with to help broaden my skillset!

I've worked as a thumbnail artist for YouTubers in the past including Alpharad and ChilledChaos.
